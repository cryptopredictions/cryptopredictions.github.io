Questions

What is CryptoPredictions?


It's a game where players make predictions with cryptocurrency, which go into pots. If your predictions turn out to be correct, you win the pots 🤑.

Who determines the winners?


Our Smart Contracts do. Each question has its own smart contract which is completely automated - from tracking crypto prices to distributing the winnings. No one, including us, can alter them. See for yourself:

  • Bitcoin $15,000 Question Smart Contract
  • Ethereum $1,000 Question Smart Contract

Can there be multiple winners?


Yes! In the case of multiple winners, the smart contracts will split the pot proportionately.

For a given question, is there a cutoff?


Yes, when a target is 90% realized. For example, "When will Ethereum pass $1000?" will be closed to all predictions when Ethereum passes $900.

Is there any advantage for making predictions well ahead of the cutoff?


The earlier you make a prediction, the higher your payout will be. We calculate the payout multiple using the number of days.
Here's an example of the winnings based on when predictions are made for a question that cuts off at 100 days and grows to a 100 ETH pot:

Amount Day of Prediction Normal Payout Actual Weighted Payout
1 ETH 1 25 ETH 40 ETH
1 ETH 10 25 ETH 35 ETH
1 ETH 50 25 ETH 20 ETH
1 ETH 100 25 ETH 5 ETH

Can I make multiple predictions?


Yes! Each prediction is tracked separately.

How do I collect my winnings?


As soon as a prediction is won, you'll receive an email notification. If your prediction was correct, you'll see a 'Redeem Winnings' button directly below the relevant question. Just click that, and your winnings will be sent to your MetaMask (see below) wallet.

What happens if no one makes the correct prediction?


This is extremely unlikely, but in the event that no one wins the pot - it'll get rolled over to the next prediction.

Is this legal?


CryptoPredictions is based out of Costa Rica, where games like this are perfectly legal.

What do I need to make predictions?


  • Desktop version of Chrome or Firefox
  • MetaMask
  • Ether

What is MetaMask? Why do I need it?


MetaMask is a digital wallet. You can install it here. You will then need to add Ether to it before you can make predictions.

How do I add Ether to my MetaMask wallet?


If you already own Ether, you can send it from your current wallet to your MetaMask wallet's address.
If you don't currently own Ether, search here.

Why Ether? Can I pay for predictions with Bitcoin?


Bitcoin transactions can take hours. Ether transactions take minutes, sometimes seconds. So predictions can only be made with Ether, for now.

Are there any fees?


5% of every prediction amount. For instance, if you make a prediction with 1 ETH, 0.95 ETH goes into the pot, and 0.05 ETH goes to buying our dog his favorite treats.
